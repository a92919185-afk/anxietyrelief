Pulsetto uses clinically validated electrical impulses to stimulate the vagus nerve through the skin on your neck. The companion app guides sessions for stress, sleep, focus, or pain relief.

Is vagus nerve stimulation safe?

Yes. Vagus nerve stimulation has been studied extensively in clinical settings for over a decade. Pulsetto uses non-invasive, gentle electrical pulses backed by peer-reviewed research. The sensation is a light, pleasant tingling. No known serious side effects when used as directed. Consult your physician if you have an implanted cardiac device.
